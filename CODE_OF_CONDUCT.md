# Code of Conduct

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and welcoming community for everyone who interacts with Energy Credit (ENE).

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Showing respect and empathy toward others  
- Providing constructive feedback  
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when mistakes occur  

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Use of sexualized or discriminatory language or imagery  
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insults  
- Public or private harassment
